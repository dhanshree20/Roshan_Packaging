About Us

Founded in 2000 by Mr. Roshan Shah, Roshan Packaging is a leading manufacturer and die-cut solutions provider based in Pune, India. With over 20 years of experience, we serve industries including solar, packaging, automotive, printing, and more.

We specialize in high-performance adhesive and die-cut products designed for superior durability, adhesion, and reliability. Committed to quality and innovation, we deliver customized solutions with timely service and dedicated technical support.
